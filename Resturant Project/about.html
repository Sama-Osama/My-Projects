Our Story

PaPa Restaurant specializes in mouthwatering chicken dishes that are

freshly prepared every day. Founded in 2010, we have grown from a small

neighborhood restaurant to a beloved spot known for our delicious menu,

excellent service, and inviting atmosphere. Whether you're in the mood for

grilled, fried, or roasted chicken, PaPa Restaurant has something for

everyone.
